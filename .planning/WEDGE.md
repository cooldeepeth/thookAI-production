# ThookAI Wedge v1 — LinkedIn Authentic-Voice Posts

## The one-sentence spec

ThookAI v1 helps non-native-English founders building in public generate LinkedIn posts in their authentic voice — so they build audience without hiring a ghostwriter or sounding like ChatGPT.

## Why this wedge (founder-market fit)

- The founder (Kuldeep) is himself a non-native-English founder building in public. Founder-market fit.
- Existing persona/voice-matching agents (scout, writer, qc, anti-repetition) are the strongest differentiator in the codebase vs. Jasper / Buffer / Opus Clip.
- LinkedIn text-only eliminates ~80% of the broken feature surface (video, voice, image, carousel, cross-platform).
- "Doesn't sound like ChatGPT" is a crisp, demoable value prop that every AI content tool is failing at today.

## In scope (v1)

### User flow

1. Register (email/password + Google OAuth)
2. Onboarding: paste 10 existing LinkedIn posts OR connect LinkedIn OAuth to fetch automatically
3. Persona extraction (scout + writer agents)
4. Content Studio: topic input → generate → edit → preview (LinkedIn shell)
5. Schedule OR publish-now to LinkedIn
6. View published post history

### Backend agents active

- commander, scout, thinker, writer, qc, anti_repetition
- All others flagged off

### Platforms

- LinkedIn only (OAuth + publish + token refresh)

### Billing

- Single tier: $19/mo
- 500 credits/month
- Costs: Content create = 10 credits. No other costs in v1 (no image, no video, no voice).
- Stripe live mode

### Output formats

- Text posts only (no images attached in v1)

## Success criteria

The wedge is validated when ALL are true:

- 10 paying users at $19/mo
- Weekly retention ≥ 60% (users who generated a post in week N generate one in week N+1)
- NPS ≥ 30 from a short in-app survey
- Sentry: zero unresolved errors for 7 consecutive days
- At least 3 users have published ≥ 5 posts generated by ThookAI

## Kill criteria (pivot signal)

Pivot the wedge (narrow further or change target) if after 8 weeks:

- Fewer than 3 paying users
- Weekly retention < 30%
- Users consistently request the same missing feature and decline to pay without it
- Technical debt preventing ship velocity

## Non-negotiable constraints

- No feature added without citation from a paying user or failed conversion
- No new wedge started until this one hits success criteria
- No merges without the 3 Playwright tests + eval harness green
