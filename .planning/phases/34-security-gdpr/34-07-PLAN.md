Gate the PostHog analytics initialization behind cookie consent by modifying the `index.html` script block to check `localStorage` before calling `posthog.init()`, and updating `CookieConsent.jsx`'s accept handler to retroactively initialize PostHog for new visitors who click Accept.

Output: `frontend/public/index.html` with consent-gated `posthog.init()`. `CookieConsent.jsx` with retroactive `posthog.init()` call on Accept for new visitors.
